>an area with a wall or bars around it that is joined to the outside wall of a building on an upper level

>a narrow floor that is attached to the outside wall of a building above the ground, usually with sides or bars, or an area of seats at an upper level in a theater

1. noun
2. "We had a drink on the hotel balcony."
3. "Our hotel room has a balcony that looks out over the pool."
